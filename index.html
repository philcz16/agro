About Us

Benue State is blessed with the greatest agricultural potential in Nigeria and possibly Africa. It has an abundance of constantly flowing rivers and annual rainfall, however very few of the resources are utilised to the benefit of the communities in the rural areas. It is only the cities that have an infrastructure due to the fact of the investments made in the past and have access to electricity supply. Supply to these areas is often interrupted and unreliable. Benue State was initially one of the largest producers of agriculture in Nigeria, thus the name The Food Basket of the Nation. Read More

  • What we can do

Galaxy Orion Agro Industries Limited has embarked upon to assist local rice farmers in the following ways

  • Train them on the new technologies in the production of rice.
  • Assist them in the modern technologies by supplying them the seedlings and also assisting them in planting the seedlings every quarter.
  • To assist them in harvesting the rice using the modern technologies thus ensuring minimum breakage in yields.
  • MAI MAI will then purchase the rice from the local communities at competitive pricing for parboiling and bagging.
  • A training centre will be established for constant education in modern technology based farming methods and will be certified. The training programming entails updated methods in the layout of the land, irrigation and various techniques of agriculture.
