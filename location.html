Location

Benue State is land locked and is surrounded by the Taraba State, Nasarrawa State, Kogi State, Enugu State, Ebonyi State and most importantly Cross River State which has a sea port in the event of export sales. This is the future plan. It is approximately 5 hours drive from the Commercial State of Lagos and about 5 hours drive from the Administrative State Abuja. Benue State is situated entirely within the tropics (see map). It is located between 70 20ⁿ and 80 45ⁿ north of the Equator. It lies south of Nasarrawa and Taraba State, East of Koji State and bounded by Chad in the East and in the south. Benue State has 16 Local Government Councils. By 1991 Census, the population of Benue State was 5,181,642 while the estimated population upon its creation on October 1st 1996 was put at 7,750,000 with the capital located at Makurdi, Benue. Mainly at Benue zone rising over 250 meters above sea level, Benue has a rhythmically undulating surface. The landscape consists of ancient plains broken by steep-sided outcropping dome rocks.
